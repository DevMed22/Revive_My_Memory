importance of video call

Video conferencing is a communication method between one or more parties via the internet. For much of its history, such online meetings were made via special app platforms, requiring both parties to invest in specialized proprietary hardware and install specialized software.
The latest iteration of this mode of communication, however, allows parties to communicate via easily downloadable apps or even ordinary web browsers through the use of WebRTC standards. As a tool, video conferencing has gained traction in almost all fields, including the healthcare field. More and more medical professionals and their patients are using web conferencing for their interactions.
